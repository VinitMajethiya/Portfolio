The Journey So Far

I operate in the spaces where logic meets human-centric design. My work is driven by the belief that complex systems should not feel complicated. They should feel intuitive.

I became interested in machine learning the same way I got into guitar — I heard something that didn't make sense to me, and I couldn't leave it alone until it did. From my first lines of Python to training deep models, my path in tech has been driven by pure curiosity.

Currently focused on applied machine learning and interactive interfaces, I'm building tools that augment capability without adding cognitive load.
